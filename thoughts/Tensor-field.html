a function assign a tensor to each point of a region of a mathematical space (typically a Euclidean space or a manifold )

Definition

Let M M M be a manifold, for instance the Euclidean plane R n \mathbb{R}^n R n

Then a tensor field of type ( p , q ) (p, q) ( p , q ) is a section

T ∈ Γ ( M , V ⊗ p ⊗ ( V ∗ ) ⊗ q ) T \in \Gamma(M, V^{\otimes p} \otimes (V^{*})^{\otimes q}) T ∈ Γ ( M , V ⊗ p ⊗ ( V ∗ ) ⊗ q )

where V V V is a vector bundle on M M M , V ∗ V^{*} V ∗ is its dual and ⊗ \otimes ⊗ is the tensor product of vector bundles

via coordinate transitions
